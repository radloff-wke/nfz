We believe you shouldn't have to choose between a mosquito-free yard and a healthy planet. Our traps use only natural materials that are safe for the whole neighborhood ecosystem.

Everything you want to know about No Fly Zone

I am out of the service zone but I would still like some buckets. Do you have any options for me?
Yes, absolutely! We offer a DIY Mosquito Larva Trap Kit for $25, which contains everything you need to service the trap yourself, and comes with instructions and monthly email reminders to ensure that you keep your trap(s) effective. This is also a great option if you want to pay less for the traps.
I am just outside of your service zone. Can you expand it just a little bit for me and my neighbors?
Sadly, we cannot. We aim to be more eco-friendly by not using cars, and since this is a kid-run business, unfortunately our parents do not want us going too far or crossing some busy streets. However, you can order a DIY bucket, and always feel free to ask us about this!
How many buckets do I need for my house?
That depends on the size of your house! These traps tend to cover a 20–80 foot radius. We've found that single-family home residents tend to opt for three to five traps, depending on the property size and mosquito intensity, while duplex residents often opt for two or three traps, and those who own a townhouse often find that they just have room for one or two. Don't let that stop you from ordering — you are absolutely welcome to take a guess on the order form, and just ask us what we think!
What do these traps look like? Will they be in the way when we are in our yard?
The traps use standard black 3.5-gallon buckets. We place them behind bushes and shrubs or up close to a fence and out of the way. This is good for both mosquitoes and people.
